About this study

We are researchers at the Interdisciplisnary Transformation University Austria. In this laboratory study, we are interested in how people make decisions in simple tasks. The study will take around 15 minutes.

You will receive a base payment for taking part. In addition, you can earn bonus points depending on your decisions during the task. Some of your decisions will also generate points for a charity.

Your participation is anonymous. Your data will be stored under an ID code that does not reveal your identity. Aggregate results may be used for scientific publications and shared in anonymized form with other researchers.

Participation is completely voluntary. You may stop at any time.

If you have any questions about this study, you can contact us at socialdynamics@it-u.at.
