Instructions

On each round, your task is simply to indicate which symbol appeared more often in a grid: O or I.

There are {{ total_rounds }} such rounds in total. After each grid, you will be asked whether you saw more O or more I symbols.

Each grid is a 7 × 7 square (49 positions) filled with O and I symbols. Here is an example of what a grid might look like:

On each round, you will first see a fixation cross in the center of the screen for 3 seconds. Immediately after that, the grid will appear for 2 seconds before disappearing.

After the grid disappears, you will be asked to report whether there were more Os or more Is.
